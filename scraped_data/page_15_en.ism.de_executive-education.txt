# Bachelor in management studies

The ISM business management programs offer you valuable education that perfectly combines practice and internships with theory. You will not only gain in-depth knowledge in the core areas of business management, but also valuable insights through our international networks and university partnerships. Get to know us and find out more about the opportunities to get a Bachelor in management studies at one of Germany's leading business universities of applied sciences.

## What are management studies?

Management studies teach how businesses and organizations are run. They cover leadership, decision-making, finance, marketing, and teamwork to help people manage resources and solve problems effectively. Many students choose a business management study courses to acquire the necessary knowledge and practical skills to thrive in the global economy. .

**What does a business manager do?** As an international business manager, you will be confident in negotiations, have a firm grasp of budgets, take on management tasks and work together in intercultural teams at home or abroad. You will also develop a profound understanding of cultural issues and are an expert in business contexts.

---

## Is a bachelor in management studies right for me?

To see if a Bachelor in Management Studies is right for you, **ask yourself:**

• Do you enjoy taking on leadership roles? – Managing teams and making decisions interests you.

• Are you good at problem-solving? – You like analyzing situations and finding solutions to complex problems.

• Do you have strong communication skills? – You enjoy presenting your ideas and working with people.

• Are you interested in business? – You’re curious about how companies operate.

• Do you like numbers and data? – Finance, budgeting, and statistics don’t scare you.

• Do you prefer a flexible career path? – You want job options in various industries.

If most of these sound like you, business management studies could be the best choice for you.

**Still wondering what the right study programme for you is?** [Our study advisors](/study-advisors) can help you decide whether a management degree on-campus or online is right for you, what you can expect in terms of content, what the admission requirements are and what career opportunities you will have afterwards.

## Entry requirements and deadlines for business management studies

#### Documents needed to apply

For the pre-check of your qualification for a business management degree program at ISM, please submit the following to the Bachelor study advisor at your preferred campus:

- **School-leaving certificate** (only if already available)
- **Transcript of records** (translated in English)
- **Brief curriculum vitae** (CV)
- Optional, only if available: certificate of language skills, for example TOEFL, IELTS or other.
- **Copy of passport or national ID card**

Upon examination and successful verification of your documents, you will be able to register for an online admission test.

[#### Application period and timeframes

We do not have any strict application dates, but **international** **applicants who require visa for Germany** must submit their application documents **by July 15th**for the programs that start in fall and **by December 15th** for the ones in spring.

**The admission test is valid for 1 year**, so we recommend that you start applying even before having your final school leaving certificate in order to save time and secure yourself early-bird study free discounts of up to 1,500 Euro. Please get informed from your country' consulate how long the study visa application process currently takes and prepare acordingly.

## How long does a bachelor of business management take to complete?

You can complete your **Business** **Bachelor full-time** in **6 semesters** and earn **180 ECTS credit points**.

Also, during the business undergraduate degree, you have the opportunity to complete an **additional seventh semester overseas** if you pick the **"Global Track"**. This option is elective and you will graduate with 210 ECTS credit points. For more information, contact the [Bachelor study advisors](/prospective-students/faqs).

## Are the ISM business management study programs accredited?

International School of Management (ISM) is a private, state-recognized and internationally AACSB-accredited University of Applied Sciences established over 35 years ago (in 1990) and that has seven campuses in **the top German cities in terms of population and business opportunities**. For years, the ISM has been featured at the top of various university rankings and is accredited by multiple state and regulatory agencies.

#### Accreditations

All our study programs meet all the strict quality requirements of the **FIBAA** (Foundation for International Business Administration Accreditation).

ISM is since 2024 **internationally accredited by AACSB**, being the first private German university of applied sciences to be honored at this highest level.

**Unlimited state recognition**: ISM is recognized by the Ministry of Science of North Rhine-Westphalia for an unlimited period of time.

Accreditation through the **German Council of Science and Humanities.** All degree programs also bear the seal of quality of **the German Accreditation Council.**

All ISM online distance learning programs bear the **ZFU** seal of quality.

## Career prospects with a bachelor of business management

Management graduates are highly valued in the international working world due to their extensive management knowledge, practical insights and intercultural skills. Regardless of whether you want to work in large international corporations, medium-sized companies or innovative start-ups in your region – a business management degree prepares you for **a wide range of jobs and industries.**

Management studies open the door to a career that is not only international, but also interdisciplinary. You will be qualified for an international business career and also equipped with a global mindset, which is very important these days. Study business management now and unlock countless career paths, from global strategy development to international marketing.

Upon successful completion of a Bachelor in business or management, you can work in a large variety of management positions **in all fields of business management**.

Business and management graduates can pursue careers in areas such as

- business development,
- company management
- global or digital marketing,
- supply chain management
- human resource management
- international finance, among others.

Whether you work for a global corporation, a medium-sized company or a start-up - your skills and abilities as an international business manager are in demand everywhere!
