## About ISM Academy

With our ISM Academy we empower professionals and organizations to achieve their highest potential. With a rich history of academic excellence and a strong commitment to practical, real-world learning, ISM Academy offers a unique opportunity for leaders to enhance their skills and drive their careers forward. Our programs are designed to cater the evolving needs of today’s dynamic business environment, providing you with the tools and knowledge necessary for success.

ISM Academy is a 100% owned subsidiary of ISM University of Applied Sciences. ISM has received the prestigious **AACSB accreditation** and is **currently the only private university of applied sciences in Germany** to be honored at the highest level by the AACSB (Association to Advance Collegiate Schools of Business).

[Learn more](https://ism-academy.de/en/executive-education "Executive education programs")

---

## What we offer

Flexible Learning Formats

Whether you prefer online learning or an immersive on-campus experience, ISM Academy offers flexible formats that suit your schedule. Join us at any of our seven locations in Germany or participate from the comfort of your home or office.

Tailored Executive Programs

Our Executive Education programs are designed to meet the specific needs of your organization. We work closely with your team to develop customized training solutions that align with your strategic goals.

Global Network and Top-Ranked Institution

Learn from a distinguished faculty with extensive industry experience and gain practical insights from our business experts. Our diverse learning environment fosters global perspectives and cross-cultural understanding.

---

## Key Features of Our Executive Education Programs

- **Innovative Courses**: Cutting-edge courses designed to address current business challenges.
- **Experienced Faculty**: Learn from our excellent lecturers, industry experts and thought leaders.
- **Networking Opportunities**: Connect with peers and industry leaders.
- **Practical Application**: Hands-on learning that you can immediately apply to your organization.
- **Accreditation**: Choose us as a university of applied sciences whose teaching is characterised by the best accreditations worldwide, such as the AACSB accreditation.
