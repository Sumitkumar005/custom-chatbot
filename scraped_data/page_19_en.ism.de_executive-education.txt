# Career Center

Whether career advice, application coaching or company databases - the Career Center provides you with the best possible support in preparing for job interviews and career paths. Through career and company events, excursions or company workshops, the Career Center offers you the ideal platform for an exchange between theory and practice.

Let us advise you

#### Application coaching & career advice

The Career Center team offers students tailored coaching, CV checks and assessment center training to prepare them optimally for their practical phase and career entry.

Application know-how

#### Workshops & Lectures

To support the practical orientation of the course, we regularly cooperate with companies for workshops and guest lectures in and outside of lectures.

We provide information about companies from a wide range of sectors as well as current topics, entry opportunities and career paths and offer a unique opportunity for networking.

ISM Network

#### ISM company database & alumni network

Once ISM - always ISM. Thanks to our long-standing relationships with companies and close contact with alumni, we offer students access to an exclusive network where, in addition to job opportunities, they can reflect on their own careers and exchange ideas with like-minded people.

Practical experience

#### Job advertisements & experience

On our job platform JobTeaser, we offer students current job advertisements.

Our internship database offers students a unique insight into everyday internship life from the perspective of a fellow student with internship experience reports from over 8,000 companies worldwide.

ISM International

#### Career Services for international students

For international students, we offer advice tailored specifically to your needs to help you prepare for a successful career start.

Career Events

#### ISM Career Day

Such events offer students direct access to company representatives. This allows them to make personal contact with companies during their studies and find out about entry opportunities and recruiting processes at an early stage.
