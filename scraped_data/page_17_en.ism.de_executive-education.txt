# Book an Online Consultation

You can book a free study consultation with our study advisors online below. They are happy to answer all your questions about the ISM, our study programs, application and selection process and financing options in a personal appointment. This usually takes place online or - on demand - on campus in Germany, as you prefer. Upon filling out the form, you will contacted per email with possible appointment dates and you'll receive the zoom participation link.
